Overview

The MMU VR Study Group is designed to enhance the online learning experience by bringing interactivity, collaboration, and engagement to virtual education. This platform empowers users with a set of powerful tools, including study group creation, collaborative note-taking, task and to-do management, real-time chat, and video conferencing. Together, these features help students and educators stay organized, connected, and actively involved in their learning journey
